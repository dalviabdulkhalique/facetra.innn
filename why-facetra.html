Why FACETRA

FACETRA was established to bring discipline, transparency and long-term investment thinking into gemstone trading — an industry traditionally driven by opacity and speculation.

Built for Trust, Not Volume

FACETRA operates as a premium gemstone trading house focused on quality, verification and long-term value rather than high-volume retail sales. Every gemstone is evaluated for authenticity, treatment disclosure, origin relevance and market demand before being considered for trade.

Certification-First Approach

Certification is treated as a foundation, not an add-on. FACETRA prioritizes independently certified gemstones from recognized gemological laboratories wherever applicable.

  • • Independent lab verification
  • • Treatment disclosure clarity
  • • Transparent documentation

Ethical & Verified Sourcing

FACETRA works only with verified suppliers and responsible trade networks. Ethical sourcing standards are enforced across all gemstone categories we engage with.

  • • Responsible mining partners
  • • Compliance-driven trade
  • • Long-term supplier relationships

Investment Discipline

Gemstones are assessed not only for beauty but for long-term liquidity, rarity and historical market performance. Selection is guided by investment-grade criteria.

  • • Rarity-driven selection
  • • Market demand analysis
  • • Long-term appreciation focus

Our Selection Process

Each gemstone considered by FACETRA passes through a structured evaluation process designed to minimize risk and ensure transparency.

01

Source Verification

Supplier credibility, mining origin and trade compliance are verified before any gemstone is reviewed.

02

Gemological Assessment

Evaluation of cut, clarity, color, carat weight, treatment and structural integrity.

03

Certification Review

Independent lab certification is reviewed or recommended depending on trade requirements.

04

Market Viability

Liquidity, demand cycles and resale viability are analyzed before offering stones to clients.
