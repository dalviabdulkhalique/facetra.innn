Why Collect with FACETRA

FACETRA is built for collectors, jewelers and investors seeking verified, high-quality gemstones. We combine rigorous gemological assessment, ethical procurement and independent certification to ensure transparency and value preservation.

Our process begins at source — we maintain long-term relationships with verified miners and traders across Asia and Africa. Each lot is evaluated for natural color, cutting precision and treatment history. High-priority lots are submitted to independent laboratories (GIA, IGI and other reputable labs) for grading and treatment disclosure. For premium lots, we capture origin reports and chain-of-custody documentation to support provenance and long-term resale value.

Whether you are a private collector aiming to build a portfolio of investment-grade stones, a jeweler seeking exceptional centerpieces, or a family office exploring alternative assets, FACETRA provides tailored sourcing, verification and secure trade facilitation. Our team can arrange private viewings, detailed lab reports and bespoke procurement on request.
