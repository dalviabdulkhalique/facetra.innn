About FACETRA

FACETRA is a premium gemstone trading house based in Mumbai, specializing in certified natural diamonds, rubies, sapphires, emeralds and rare untreated gemstones for collectors, jewelers and long-term investors.

Who We Are

FACETRA was established to bring transparency, certification and investment-grade discipline to gemstone trading. Every stone we offer is evaluated not only for beauty, but for rarity, geological origin, treatment disclosure and long-term market value.

Authenticity First

All gemstones are independently certified by internationally recognized gemological laboratories.

Ethical Global Sourcing

We partner only with verified suppliers following responsible mining and trade compliance standards.

Investment Discipline

Selection is based on rarity, market liquidity and long-term appreciation potential.

Our Verification Process

FACETRA follows a structured multi-stage quality framework before any gemstone enters our inventory.

01

Global Sourcing

Stones are procured from trusted international supply networks and verified dealers.
